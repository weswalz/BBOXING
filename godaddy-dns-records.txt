==============================================
MIGRATION STEPS:
==============================================
1. BEFORE MIGRATION:
   - Lower TTL on Cloudflare records to 300 seconds (5 minutes)
   - Wait at least 1 hour for old TTL to expire
   - Take screenshots of current Cloudflare DNS settings

2. MIGRATION:
   - Change nameservers at GoDaddy from Cloudflare to GoDaddy's default
   - Add all records above in GoDaddy DNS management
   - Verify all records are saved

3. AFTER MIGRATION:
   - Test email immediately (send and receive)
   - Check website accessibility 
   - Monitor DNS propagation (use whatsmydns.net)
   - Test email authentication (use mail-tester.com)

==============================================
IMPORTANT NOTES:
==============================================
- Email may have brief interruptions during propagation
- DNS changes can take 24-48 hours to fully propagate
- Keep Cloudflare DNS records as backup reference
- The website will be accessible via IP during propagation
- Consider using Google's 5 MX records for better reliability
